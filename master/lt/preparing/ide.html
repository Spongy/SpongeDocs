IDE diegimas¶

Kas yra IDE?¶

The installation of an IDE - also known as an integrated development environment - is recommended for developing Sponge plugins and/or working on Sponge itself. An IDE auto-completes names and notifies you of errors, such as mismatched braces, in your code.

Kurį turėčiau pasirinkti?¶

Every IDE has its pros and cons, and there is no single best IDE for Sponge development. The IDE you choose to use is largely a matter of personal preference. That being said, it is worth mentioning that IntelliJ IDEA Community Edition is fairly popular amongst a majority of the core Sponge team.
