Optional¶

Optionals are a feature widely used in the SpongeAPI. This section tries to explain why we need them and how they’re used. If you already know why we use Optionals, just head directly to the 使用例 section. If you’re unsure what they are, what they do and why we need them, then Optional の解説 should be the first chapter to read.
